Açai Bowl

Encountered this dairy-free açai bowl recipe in hawaii. It was very good!

Ingredient list

  • 1 cup acai berry sorbet.
  • 2 tablespoons granola, or as desired.
  • 4 strawberries, sliced.
  • 1 banana, sliced.
  • 2 teaspoons unsweetened coconut flakes, or as desired.
  • 1 teaspoon honey, or as desired.

Directions

  1. Place the acai sorbet into a bowl and top with a layer of granola.
  2. Line strawberry and banana slices over granola.
  3. Sprinkle with coconut flakes and drizzle with honey.

Nutrition facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 551
  • Fat: 13g
  • Carbs: 108g
  • Protein: 4g
